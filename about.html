Solutions We've Built

Beyond marketing, we build real products. From HMRC-compliant tax platforms to full-scale project management tools, here are some of the big projects we've delivered.

01

AnyTax — Tax Filing Platform

We designed, developed, and launched AnyTax — a fully digital tax filing platform built to simplify self-assessment for freelancers, sole traders, and small businesses across the UK.

AnyTax streamlines the entire process from income entry to HMRC submission, with an intuitive interface that makes tax filing accessible to everyone, not just accountants.

Web App UX Design Tax Filing HMRC Integration
02

Making Tax Digital — MTD Compliance

With HMRC's Making Tax Digital mandate transforming how UK businesses handle VAT and income tax, we built a compliant MTD solution that bridges the gap between legacy accounting and digital-first tax reporting.

Our platform enables businesses to maintain digital records, submit quarterly updates, and stay fully compliant with HMRC's evolving requirements — all through a clean, modern interface.

MTD Compliance VAT Submissions Digital Records HMRC API
03

Project Management — Custom PM Tools

We developed bespoke project management applications tailored for teams that need more than off-the-shelf solutions. From task tracking and resource allocation to real-time collaboration and reporting dashboards.

Built for construction firms, agencies, and enterprise teams who need full visibility across projects, milestones, and team performance — without the bloat of generic PM software.
